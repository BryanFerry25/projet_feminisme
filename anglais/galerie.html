Simone de Beauvoir

1908-1986

Theorist of feminism, creator of the book The Second Sex, She participated in the women’s liberation movement in the 1970s.

Emmeline Pankhurst

1858-1928

A great British feminist who organized the British suffragette movement, she helped women in Britain to obtain the right to vote.

Marie Olympe de Gouges

1748-1793

The first major feminist activist to draft the Declaration of Women’s and Citizens' Rights.

Simone Segouin

1925 ( 96 years old )

She joined the movement at the age of 18 and has fought tirelessly since she was very young.

Rebecca Amsellem

1988( 33 years old )

A great feminist who created the Les Glorieuses newsletter. She also fights for women’s equal pay.

Great resistant who organizes the escape of several resistant. She fights for gender equality, women’s right to vote and many more.
