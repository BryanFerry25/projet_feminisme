Equality
The fact of requiring all discrimination between individuals from the point of view of rights, duties but also in all economic, political, legal, social etc.
Feminism
A movement that will take women as a part, it will focus on its rights, its duties and draw inequalities to try to equal women to men. This movement affects all the Domains whether economic, political or even legal.
Humanity
A notion which considers that solidarity must be shared with each individual. In a more general way humanity is the whole of any human being regardless of their sex, skin color etc...
Prejudice
View of an individual often linked to society, bias and time.
Resistance
A historical term that refers to a group of individuals who refuse to allow their country to be occupied and who will therefore form their own group in order to eliminate foreign threats.
Salary
System of remuneration obtained when one goes to work or to do a service.
Second World War
Period of war qualified as World War and taking place in 1939 to 1945 . The Allies and the Axis clashed, causing between 50 and 70 million deaths worldwide.
Sexuality
This concept will include all phenomena that will be related or indirectly related to sex, with for example the species reproduction, the rapprochement between two bodies of individuals or even the sexual instinct leading to satisfaction.
Society
It is a group of individuals who live together.
