ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Bryan Ferry, the creator of this website.

I am a student in the first year of the AIM MMI (Multimedia and Internet Business) in Montbéliard.

I am passionate about design and digital creation, but I also like everything about new technologies and of course the web in general.

This website is a project carried out as part of an educational exercise within the institution.

The project consists of creating a website optimized for Iphone X in which we will talk about feminism, a topical topic but which goes back a long time already.

I chose to present several themes including feminism during the war, feminism in the economy and the prejudices of women which are a fundamental point of feminism.
